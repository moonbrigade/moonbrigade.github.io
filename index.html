About Us

We are a multi gaming guild that has spanned across many mmos. Our favorites are survival sandbox games (H1Z1, ARK, Rust, etc etc), but we play a lot more mmos than just that. We stay together through our various groups primarily through our discord. We're always playing a lot of games and are always open to more, the current favorites are overwatch, ARK, and Albion. We've been together through a lot of mmos and play to stay through more so even when more mmos release you bet Moon Brigade will be there. We like to have fun, but we also take our gaming seriously, we strive to be the best but we take time to enjoy ourselves and have some fun. We're also avid anime watchers, we have a chat channel specifically for anime, and other popular tv shows (game of thrones, walking dead, etc etc) its nice on a sunday night when the guild takes a break kicks back and watches some game of thrones and then chat and discuss it after while kicking some ass in whatever game we're playing.
